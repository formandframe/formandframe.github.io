Frequently asked questions

Why develop video content?

Video is the new means of online communication. With every major platform pushing video (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube) consumers are not only more accustomed to consuming content this way, but it now dramatically outperforms image or text-based content.

Is video cost effective?

Video, due to its high engagement and conversion rates, is one of the most cost-effective and impactful ways to reach new clients.

Those who invest in a long-term strategy. not only reach customers at a fraction of what it would cost with traditional print, image or text-based advertising, they're able to build deeper connections and a degree of brand leadership that simply isn't possible with any other medium.

This allows for the ability to generate more leads, spend less time selling, and charge more per project.

Who is video for?

Video is best for an established organization that is looking to solidify its authority and prioritize long-term growth.

Organizations who are able to invest can dramatically outperform competitors due to increased awareness, trust, and social proof.
